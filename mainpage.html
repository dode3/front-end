HIFA Home Interactive Friendly Assistant






HIFA

HIFA is a short for home interactive friendly assistant.It's a voice control system that lets you speak your wishes to an echo smart speaker and see them fulfilled like turn on/off your lights, playing music tracks and even setting alarms.It can also control several smart devices using itself as a home automation system.There is no activation button to press simply say the trigger word followed by what you want to happen .
